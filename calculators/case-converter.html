Case Converter

Convert your text to UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, or Sentence case.

Formula

UPPERCASE: Convert all letters to capital letters.
lowercase: Convert all letters to small letters.
Title Case: Capitalize the first letter of each word.
Sentence Case: Capitalize the first letter of each sentence.

Example

Input: "hello world. this is toolmint!"
UPPERCASE → "HELLO WORLD. THIS IS TOOLMINT!"
lowercase → "hello world. this is toolmint!"
Title Case → "Hello World. This Is Toolmint!"
Sentence case → "Hello world. This is toolmint!"
