Age Calculator

Enter your birth date to calculate your age.

Formula

Age = Current Year − Birth Year (adjust if birthday hasn’t occurred yet this year)

Example

If your birth date is 15-03-2000 and today is 07-12-2025: Age = 2025 − 2000 = 25 (since birthday passed this year)
