BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) in Metric or Imperial units.

Formula

Metric: BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ (Height (m))²
Imperial: BMI = [Weight (lbs) ÷ (Height (in))²] × 703

Example

Metric: Weight = 70 kg, Height = 175 cm → BMI ≈ 22.86 (Normal weight)
Imperial: Weight = 154 lbs, Height = 5 ft 9 in → BMI ≈ 22.73 (Normal weight)
